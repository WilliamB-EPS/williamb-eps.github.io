Fusor

Welcome to fusor! In this club, we are working on constructing a nuclear fusion reactor. It runs afterschool on Tuesdays and Wedndesdays. The club also several breakout groups, including groups for management, gas injection, and more! It is currently run by Ms. Lewellen, who is Eastside Preparatory School's technology teacher.
